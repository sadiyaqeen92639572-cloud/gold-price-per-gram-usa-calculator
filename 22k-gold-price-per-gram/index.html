22K Gold in Wedding & Bridal Jewelry

22K gold is the traditional standard for South Asian and Middle Eastern-American wedding jewelry — bridal sets, dowry pieces and festival gold in immigrant and diaspora communities across the US are almost always this karat, prized for its rich yellow color and high gold content while still being workable enough for intricate designs. Many US jewelers serving these communities stock 22K specifically alongside the more typical American 14K/10K lines.
